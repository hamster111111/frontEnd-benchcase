ABOUT US

BSCTA is a leading aviation company connecting skilled professionals with opportunities across the global aviation industry. With over 12 years of experience, we've established ourselves as a trusted partner for both aviation professionals and employers.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between exceptional talent and aviation companies, fostering growth and innovation in the industry. We specialize in a wide range of aviation positions including:

  • Pilots and flight crew
  • Aircraft maintenance technicians
  • Air traffic controllers
  • Aviation management professionals
  • Ground operations specialists

Through our advanced matching technology, we ensure that candidates find positions that align with their career goals, while helping companies discover professionals who will contribute to their success. Our dedication to excellence and personalized service has made us the preferred choice for aviation recruitment worldwide.
